Our Story

D.L. Veranoâ€™s Electrical Supply and Services was founded in October 1985 by Engr. Dante L. Verano to provide electrical supplies, train aspiring electricians, and help address unemployment. Starting as a small retail shop, it grew into contracting, focusing on quality and affordable services, completing over 12,000 residential units and numerous commercial/industrial projects. In 2022, Dante passed the business to his sons, Dante Leonardo Verano and Dan Christian Cortez, who rebranded it in 2023 as DLV Electrical Services Co. They expanded services to include energy-efficient lighting, modern installations, and automation, positioning the company as an industry leader. Today, DLV continues to thrive, embracing technology and sustainability while maintaining its founding values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.
